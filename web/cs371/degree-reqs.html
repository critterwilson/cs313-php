Degree Requirements

Religion Credit Policy All Degree-Seeking Students

Students must complete the required cornerstone courses listed in the tables below. In addition, students must take additional REL courses to complete the minimum number of required REL credits at BYU-Idaho.

Non-Transfer Bachelors and Associates Degree-Seeking Students

The BYU-Idaho Catalog states that “[a] student is responsible for completing the General Education (Foundations for catalog years prior to 2019) and Major requirements listed in any one BYU–Idaho Catalog (chosen by the student) from the year of first enrollment to the year of graduation, provided the BYU–Idaho Catalog chosen is not more than seven years old.” In most cases, the religion credit requirements for degree-seeking students who do not have any transfer credits are as follows:

Degree Type

Minimum Required Religion Courses

 

Bachelors

REL 200C, REL 225C, REL 250C, *REL 275C, and 6 additional REL credits

Associates

2 of (REL 200C, REL 225C, REL 250C, *REL 275C) and 4 additional REL credits

Please be sure to check each student’s respective catalog (in the proper catalog year) to verify that these requirements are accurate.

* Student who have taken both REL 121 and REL 122 will not be required to take REL 275C.
